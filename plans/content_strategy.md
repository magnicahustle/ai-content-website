# Content Strategy - Phase 1

## Objective
Generate high-quality content to attract organic traffic. This content will be the foundation for future monetization efforts like affiliate marketing and advertising.

## Target Audience
- Tech enthusiasts
- AI and Machine Learning developers
- People interested in automation

## Content Plan
1.  **Topic Generation:** Initially, focus on topics related to AI, automation, and programming.
2.  **Content Creation:** Generate one article per day.
3.  **Monetization:** Once a small library of content is built, integrate affiliate links for relevant products and services.

## Initial Topics
- "Getting Started with AI: A Beginner's Guide"
- "How to Automate Your Daily Tasks with Python"
- "The Future of AI: Trends to Watch in 2026"
